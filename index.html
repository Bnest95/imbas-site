AI is going to be making a huge amount of our decisions, or being consulted on them  —  a layer over nearly all of society. We know very little about how it thinks or behaves, and it’s changing every day. That’s what Imbas is here to help with.

what imbas is

Imbas measures what frontier AI systems surface, what they leave out, and how those patterns compound over time.

What Imbas does for you

  • AI answers shape what you see.
  • Imbas shows where the answer may have gone thin.
  • You inspect the signal.
  • You decide what matters.
  • You push further if needed.

Right now Imbas documents how these systems behave, one case at a time. Where it’s going: a tool that rides alongside your AI and flags the gap in the moment  —  so you see what’s missing while you’re reading, not after it matters.

Because you have the right to know.

III  /  How it works

What the model knows.

What it surfaces.

What it leaves out.

That difference is the Volunteer Gap.

The Volunteer Gap measures the difference between what an AI model gives you from an open question and what it gives you when asked directly.

If a model can surface a named mechanism when prompted directly but does not include it in a related open answer, Imbas records the gap.
