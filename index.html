AI is becoming a layer over decision-making. Consulted, trusted, repeated, built into workflows. We know very little about how these systems behave — and they change every day.

Imbas measures what frontier AI systems surface, what they leave out, and how those patterns compound.

That is what Imbas is here to measure.

What the model knows.

What it surfaces.

What it leaves out.

That difference is the Volunteer Gap.

The Volunteer Gap measures the difference between what an AI model gives you from an open question and what it gives you when asked directly.

If a model can surface a named mechanism when prompted directly but does not include it in a related open answer, Imbas records the gap.
