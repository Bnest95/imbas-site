For Readers

You see the answer. You do not automatically see what was left out.

AI answers increasingly shape what people see, search, write, and decide. Imbas helps readers inspect where an answer may have gone thin.

What Imbas helps you notice

  • Missing named mechanisms — regulations, sources, or events the model did not mention until asked directly.
  • One-sided framing — an answer that emphasizes one perspective while omitting context that would complicate it.
  • Deflection — a response that redirects away from the concern rather than addressing it.

How to use the archive

  1. Read a case — start with the open prompt and finding.
  2. Compare open and targeted prompts side by side.
  3. Inspect the Volunteer Gap score and what it means in plain language.
  4. Decide what matters for your question; Imbas records behavior, not verdicts.
