Institutions

For Institutions

Not another model to trust. A way to check the ones you already use.

Regulated teams are deploying AI faster than they can verify how it behaves. Imbas creates independent, cross-vendor records of what systems surface and leave out.

What Imbas can support

  • Independent behavioral measurement across frontier models
  • Cross-model comparison under documented prompt conditions
  • Citable case records for audit and review
  • Audit-ready documentation of observed outputs
  • Public-interest review of surfacing patterns

What Imbas does not claim

  • Does not infer model intent
  • Does not replace internal governance or compliance programs
  • Does not certify model safety
  • Records observed behavior under documented prompts only

Near-term deliverables

  • Case records with scoring rationale
  • Methodology notes and version history
  • Archive reports as the record grows
  • Sample documentation for review workflows
  • Eventually, live signal tooling
